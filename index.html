Common questions

Is my data secure? expand_more
Yes. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest on AWS servers. We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and LGPD. No ads, no tracking, no data selling. Ever.
How does the AI receipt scanner work? expand_more
Take a photo of any receipt. Our AI extracts the total, each line item, discounts, taxes, merchant name, and even the currency — automatically. You review and confirm.
Can I use it for trips with friends? expand_more
Yes. Use "Expense Split" mode for trips — each person pays with their own money, and HomePot calculates who owes whom at the end, minimizing the number of transfers.
How much does it cost? expand_more
Free plan: 1 group, 20 expenses/month, 10 AI scans/month, multi-currency, no ads. Pro: unlimited everything from €2.92/month (annual). That's 15x less than a coffee per day.
How does HomePot compare to Splitwise? expand_more
HomePot is built as an honest, ad-free alternative: 52 currencies for expense entry, AI receipt scanning that reads any currency from receipts, and a transparent subscription model. See the full side-by-side comparison with Splitwise for every feature and a disclaimer about our sources.
