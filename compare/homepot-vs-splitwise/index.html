Frequently asked questions

Can I import my Splitwise data into HomePot? expand_more
CSV import is on the roadmap. For now, you can export your Splitwise group as CSV and we'll add an importer soon. Contact us at homepotapp@gmail.com and we'll notify you the day it ships.
Is HomePot really free? expand_more
Yes. The free plan includes 1 group, 20 expenses per month, 10 AI receipt scans, multi-currency, and zero ads — forever. Pro (from €2.92/mo annual) removes limits and adds unlimited groups, exports, and advanced analytics.
How does HomePot make money if there are no ads? expand_more
Subscriptions. Pro costs €2.92/mo on annual billing — less than a coffee per month. That's the only revenue model. No ads, no data selling, no affiliate deals inside the app.
Does HomePot work offline? expand_more
Yes. Add expenses offline; they sync automatically when you reconnect. Settlement calculations run on-device, so you can see balances without a connection.
What currencies does HomePot support? expand_more
14 currencies with automatic detection from receipts: EUR, USD, GBP, CHF, CAD, AUD, NZD, JPY, SEK, KRW, PLN, BRL, MXN, CNY. Exchange rates update daily.
Is HomePot GDPR compliant? expand_more
Yes. We comply with GDPR (EU), CCPA (California), and LGPD (Brazil). Data is hosted on AWS EU, encrypted at rest and in transit. Full account and data deletion in one tap from Settings.
