What makes HomePot different

Every feature built around one promise: no ads, no tracking, no friction.

document_scanner

AI reads every line of the receipt

Our scanner doesn't stop at the total. It extracts each item, tax, discount, and tip so you can split a restaurant bill exactly the way the group consumed it — not by a forced even share.

currency_exchange

52 currencies + AI reads any receipt

52 currencies supported for expense entry, covering Europe, the Americas, Asia, the Middle East, Oceania and Africa. The AI scanner goes further and detects any ISO 4217 currency from the receipt image, then auto-converts to your group's base currency at live exchange rates.

block

Zero ads, zero trackers — forever

We make money from optional subscriptions, not from your attention or your data. No interstitial ads, no cooldown timers, no third-party analytics SDKs. This is a written, verifiable policy — not a marketing tagline.

tune

Fixed vs variable split in the same group

Rent, internet and insurance split evenly between everyone. Dinner and groceries split only between the people who were there. HomePot models both cases natively in the same group — no workarounds, no separate spreadsheets.

auto_awesome

Monthly close with minimum transfers

At the end of the month, HomePot computes the smallest set of transfers needed to bring every balance back to zero. Instead of chains of small payments between many people, you get one or two clean settlements and the month is closed.

shield

Real privacy, documented in plain language

A public GDPR-compliant policy, data hosted on AWS EU, one-tap account and data deletion from Settings. Nothing hidden in fine print and nothing sold to data brokers.

Frequently asked questions

Can I import my Splitwise data into HomePot? expand_more
CSV import is on our roadmap. In the meantime you can export your Splitwise group as CSV and a HomePot importer will land soon. Write to homepotapp@gmail.com and we'll notify you the day it ships.
Is HomePot really free? expand_more
Yes. The free plan includes 1 group, 20 expenses per month, 10 AI receipt scans, multi-currency, and zero ads — forever. Pro starts at €2.92/month on annual billing and removes the limits, plus adds unlimited groups, exports and advanced analytics.
What exactly do I get on HomePot's free tier, without paying? expand_more
A free HomePot account gives you: 1 group with unlimited members, 20 expenses per month, a limited number of AI receipt scans per month, receipt photo uploads, the full 52-currency picker, charts and spending analytics, recurring expenses, fixed and variable splitting in the same group, the monthly close with minimum-transfer settlement, dark mode, and zero ads. Pro mainly removes the group/expense/scan limits and adds CSV/PDF export for your stats.
How does HomePot make money if there are no ads? expand_more
Optional subscriptions. Pro is €2.92/month on annual billing — less than a coffee per month. That's the only revenue model. No ads, no data selling, no affiliate placements inside the app.
How many currencies does HomePot really support? expand_more
52 currencies are selectable from the picker for expense entry, covering Europe, the Americas, Asia, the Middle East, Oceania and Africa. On top of that, the AI receipt scanner detects any ISO 4217 currency directly from the receipt image — so even if your group's base currency is outside the 52, you can still scan a receipt anywhere in the world and see it auto-converted.
Is HomePot GDPR compliant? expand_more
Yes. We comply with GDPR (EU), CCPA (California), and LGPD (Brazil). Data is hosted on AWS in the EU, encrypted at rest and in transit. Complete account and data deletion is available in one tap from the Settings screen.

Please verify current pricing and features on those sources, since they can change.
